How It Works

We condense relevant sports data into daily insights to help you make better bets

Sharp Money Analysis

Sportsbooks like DraftKings track public and professional bets to identify value, with professional bettors known as "sharps" being a strong indicator.

DraftCodes automates this analysis to highlight games with sharp action, which can increase your win rates when considered alongside other betting strategies.

Historical Trend Analysis

Online sources like ESPN and SportsReference.com offer predictive percentages based on statistical metrics and averages, while bookmakers provide implied chances of winning through moneyline odds. Our algorithm identifies scenarios where these indicators deviate significantly to pinpoint matchups where one side holds value, taking into account recent trends and patterns.

Negative indicators

DraftCodes' algorithm identifies injury risks and games with high viewership that can make markets more efficient and harder to find value.

This analysis helps bettors make informed decisions when certain indicators are negative.
